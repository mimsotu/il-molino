Nestled in the beautiful hills of Montefiascone, you will find the Il Molino farm with olive trees stretching over forty hectares as we did a few years ago during a visit to the region. Il Molino is a family estate solely dedicated to growing olives and organic cultivation of food. Our aim is to bring a taste of Italy to Cambridge through our selection of award winning extra virgin olive oils produced on a single estate olive farm in Montefiasconi, Lazio, Italy.
