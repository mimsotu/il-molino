Our Products

Olive Oil

The farm harvests the olive fruit when the olives start getting dark; this is crucial for the production of a light, slightly fruity – low acidity flavoured oil. The olives are picked on company land only and pressed, within the span of 3-4-hours in the farm’s press; Il Molino olive oil is full of chlorophyll, polyphenol and tocopherol, which helps the anti-oxidant process and represent a precious source of vitamins. The anti-oxidant activity of the polyphenol and tocopherol has positive effects on cardiovascular health.

Pasta

We are used to eating the traditional white wheat flour which is obtained from a grain produced at industrial levels and modified over the years to increase the amount produced. This type of production results in a flour with poor nutritional values. For this reason we cultivate ancient grains which in Italy the best known is the Senatore Cappelli wheat. The product made from this type of grain have particularly tasty flavour and odour. They are machined by grinding stone, the flour that is produced is therefore much less refined than that produced by modern wheat, thus keeping much more nutritional properties in the grain. The reduced presence of gluten in these grains, makes products derived from it, much lighter and easier to digest. Consuming the ancient grain also helps small producers who cultivate their land with love and respect for the environment, protecting the biodiversity for future generations.

We sell a variety of pasta, including:
  • Durum Wheat Paccheri
  • Durum Wheat Spaghetti
  • Rigatoni
  • Fusilli
  • Spelt
